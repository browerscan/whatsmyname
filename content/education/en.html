What is WhatsMyName?

WhatsMyName is a search engine that finds where a specific username exists across the internet. Instead of searching for web pages, it searches for you.

The Original Project

Created in 2015 by cybersecurity expert Micah "WebBreacher" Hoffman, WhatsMyName has been used by security professionals, journalists, and investigators worldwide for open-source intelligence (OSINT). Our web app makes this technology accessible to everyone.

The tool simultaneously checks over 1,400 websites and platforms to see if a username exists, giving you a complete map of a digital identity in seconds.

Why Your Username Matters

Your username is your digital fingerprint. It links your identity across platforms, and knowing where it exists is crucial for security and privacy.

Forgotten Accounts

The average person has accounts on over 200 websites. Most can't even remember half of them. Old accounts with weak passwords are security risks.

Identity Protection

In 2024, 2.9 billion records were exposed in data breaches. Knowing where your data exists is the first step to protecting it.

Impersonation Detection

42% of users have been victims of social media identity theft. Someone could be using your username right now, pretending to be you.

Personal Audit

86% of US adults are actively trying to reduce their digital footprints. You can't reduce what you can't measure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal?
Yes. We only search publicly available information. No hacking, no accessing private data. It's the same information anyone could find by manually checking each website.
Do you store my searches?
No. Your search queries are processed and immediately discarded. We don't build profiles on users or sell data. The tool is meant to help you, not track you.
Why would I search my own username?
To find forgotten accounts, check for impersonation, audit your digital footprint, or prepare for a security cleanup. Most people are shocked by how many accounts they've created over the years.
Can I search someone else's username?
You can search any username. However, ethical use is important. This tool is intended for personal security, legitimate investigations, and research purposes—not for stalking or harassment.
Why don't some platforms show up?
Some platforms have strong anti-enumeration protections, require authentication to check usernames, or change their detection methods frequently. Our database is constantly updated, but some platforms remain difficult to check programmatically.
How accurate are the results?
"Found" results are highly accurate—we verify the username exists on that platform. "Not Found" means the username either doesn't exist or the platform doesn't allow username enumeration. Always verify important results by visiting the platform directly.
