Use Cases

Who Uses WhatsMyName?

From individuals protecting their privacy to security professionals conducting investigations, WhatsMyName serves diverse use cases across industries.

HR Professionals

Verify candidate backgrounds and professional presence across platforms during the hiring process.

Common Uses:

  • Verify LinkedIn and portfolio accounts
  • Check for professional consistency across platforms
  • Identify potential red flags in public profiles
  • Confirm claimed expertise or experience

Security Researchers

Conduct OSINT investigations and analyze digital footprints for threat intelligence.

Common Uses:

  • Map target infrastructure and accounts
  • Identify attack surface via exposed usernames
  • Track threat actors across platforms
  • Support incident response investigations

Brand Protection

Monitor brand name usage and detect impersonation or trademark infringement.

Common Uses:

  • Find unauthorized use of brand names
  • Detect impersonation accounts on social platforms
  • Protect executive and brand usernames
  • Monitor for typosquatting and similar names

Personal Privacy

Take control of your digital footprint by finding and managing old accounts.

Common Uses:

  • Find forgotten accounts to close them
  • Check for impersonation of your identity
  • Audit personal information exposure
  • Clean up digital footprint before job searches

Journalists

Verify sources and research subjects through open-source investigation techniques.

Common Uses:

  • Verify source identity claims
  • Research subject background across platforms
  • Find additional context from public profiles
  • Corroborate information from multiple sources

Developers

Claim consistent usernames across developer platforms and build a unified online presence.

Common Uses:

  • Check username availability on dev platforms
  • Maintain consistent handle across GitHub, GitLab, etc.
  • Find old code repositories from past projects
  • Audit developer portfolio presence
